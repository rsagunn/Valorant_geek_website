Agents



Agents are the characters players control during a matches. Each agent has special abilities that help them wind points for the team in different ways. There are five main types of agents each with their own role
  1. Duelists: These agents are meant for attacking and getting kills. They have abilities that help them move quickly or fight aggressively.
  2. Controllers: These agents are meant for controlling the battlefield. They have abilities that help them block off areas or control the enemy's movements.
  3. Sentinels: These agents are meant for defending. They have abilities that help them protect their team or gather information on the enemy's movements.
  4. Initiators: These agents are meant for starting fights. They have abilities that help them disrupt the enemy's plans or force them to move out of position.
