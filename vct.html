VALORANT Champions Tour (VCT)



What is VCT?


The VALORANT Champions Tour is the premier competitive circuit for VALORANT esports, featuring the world's best teams competing for the title of global champion.

Tournaments

  1. The Leagues - Americas, EMEA, Pacific

  2. Masters - International tournaments competeting to get into Champions

  3. Champions - The last step of winning VCT
