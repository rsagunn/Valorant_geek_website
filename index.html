Valorant



Valorant is a free tactical FPS game made by Riot Games where players choose from a roster of unique agents each with their own abilities to compete in team based matches. The primary goal is to either plant or defuse a bomb or eliminate the enemy team. With its emphasis on strategy precise shooting and coordination Valorant has quickly become a staple in competitive gaming and esports.
